Kuwait's First Comprehensive Innovation Ecosystem

ترابط (Tarabot) is a pioneering national project aimed at establishing the first integrated innovation ecosystem in Kuwait. It operates as a highly reliable digital platform that connects talented Kuwaiti creators and entrepreneurs with strategic capital and commercial opportunities.

🔐

Trust Engine

Mandatory identity verification through the national "Hawiyti" system, creating authenticated personal profiles that showcase user skills and achievements in a tamper-proof manner.

🤖

AI Super Agent

Personal assistant powered by artificial intelligence to multiply user productivity, helping them with everything from business plan preparation to marketing content creation.

🏪

Innovation Marketplace

Economic engine that allows creators to offer services, sell digital products, and launch crowdfunding campaigns, while investors receive a consistent flow of verified investment opportunities.

🏛️

GovTech Engine

Integrated unit to facilitate company establishment procedures and issue commercial licenses electronically, transforming ideas into operating legal entities.

User Journey: From Idea to Success

Creator Journey (7 Steps)

1. Hawiyti Verification

Secure identity verification through Kuwait's national digital identity system, establishing trust and authenticity.

2. Digital Palm Profile

Create comprehensive verified profile showcasing skills, experience, and achievements with tamper-proof digital credentials.

3. AI-Assisted Development

Leverage the AI Super Agent to refine business ideas, create business plans, and develop marketing strategies.

4. Innovation Marketplace

Launch projects, offer services, and connect with potential investors through the integrated marketplace platform.

5. Investor Matching

AI-powered matching system connects creators with compatible investors based on industry, stage, and investment criteria.

6. Legal Formation

Utilize GovTech integration for seamless business registration and legal entity formation through government partnerships.

7. Launch & Scale

Access ongoing support, mentorship, and additional funding rounds as the business grows and scales.

Investor Journey (7 Steps)

1. Verified Registration

Complete Hawiyti verification and investor accreditation process to access premium investment opportunities.

2. Investment Profile

Set investment preferences, risk tolerance, and sector interests for personalized opportunity matching.

3. Deal Flow Access

Receive curated investment opportunities from verified entrepreneurs with comprehensive due diligence materials.

4. AI-Powered Analytics

Access advanced analytics, market insights, and risk assessments powered by artificial intelligence.

5. Direct Engagement

Connect directly with entrepreneurs through secure messaging and video conferencing within the platform.

6. Investment Execution

Complete investments through integrated legal and financial infrastructure with full compliance support.

7. Portfolio Management

Monitor portfolio performance, receive regular updates, and access exit opportunities through the platform.

رحلة المستخدم: من الفكرة إلى النجاح

رحلة المبدع (7 خطوات)

1. التحقق من هويتي

التحقق الآمن من الهوية من خلال نظام الهوية الرقمية الوطني في الكويت، مما يؤسس الثقة والأصالة.

2. ملف النخلة الرقمية

إنشاء ملف شخصي شامل وموثق يعرض المهارات والخبرة والإنجازات مع أوراق اعتماد رقمية مقاومة للتلاعب.

3. التطوير بمساعدة الذكاء الاصطناعي

الاستفادة من الوكيل الذكي الفائق لتحسين أفكار الأعمال وإنشاء خطط العمل وتطوير استراتيجيات التسويق.

4. سوق الابتكار

إطلاق المشاريع وتقديم الخدمات والتواصل مع المستثمرين المحتملين من خلال منصة السوق المتكاملة.

5. مطابقة المستثمرين

نظام مطابقة مدعوم بالذكاء الاصطناعي يربط المبدعين بالمستثمرين المتوافقين بناءً على الصناعة والمرحلة ومعايير الاستثمار.

6. التكوين القانوني

استخدام تكامل التكنولوجيا الحكومية لتسجيل الأعمال السلس وتكوين الكيان القانوني من خلال الشراكات الحكومية.

7. الإطلاق والتوسع

الوصول إلى الدعم المستمر والإرشاد وجولات التمويل الإضافية مع نمو وتوسع الأعمال.

Strategic Partnership Proposal for Motawer

Executive Summary

We propose a transformative strategic partnership between ترابط (Tarabot) and the Motawer program that goes beyond traditional accelerator relationships. This partnership positions both organizations as co-architects of Kuwait's innovation ecosystem, directly supporting Vision 2035 objectives while creating a replicable model for regional expansion.

Partnership Framework

🤝

What ترابط Brings

  • Complete technology platform with AI capabilities
  • Hawiyti integration for verified user ecosystem
  • Proven business model and implementation roadmap
  • Regional expansion strategy (THEE OASIS)
  • Gender-inclusive approach supporting both male and female entrepreneurs
🏛️

What Motawer Provides

  • Institutional backing from CBK and banking sector
  • Mentorship network and industry expertise
  • Regulatory support and government connections
  • Market validation and credibility
  • Access to established entrepreneur pipeline

Mutual Value Creation

For Motawer: ترابط becomes the digital infrastructure powering your program, providing verified entrepreneur profiles, AI-enhanced due diligence, and automated progress tracking. Your alumni gain permanent verified credentials on the platform, creating lasting value beyond the program duration.

For ترابط: Motawer provides the institutional credibility, regulatory support, and initial user base needed for rapid market penetration. The partnership validates our approach and creates a sustainable pipeline of verified entrepreneurs.

Implementation Approach

Rather than participating in the traditional 5-month program cycle, we propose immediate strategic partnership implementation:

Phase 1: Partnership Agreement (Month 1)

Formalize strategic partnership, define roles and responsibilities, establish governance structure.

Phase 2: Platform Integration (Months 2-3)

Integrate Motawer branding and processes into ترابط platform, onboard existing alumni network.

Phase 3: Joint Launch (Month 4)

Public launch of enhanced platform with Motawer endorsement, begin processing new applications.

Phase 4: Scale & Expand (Months 5-12)

Scale operations, measure impact, prepare for regional expansion phase.
